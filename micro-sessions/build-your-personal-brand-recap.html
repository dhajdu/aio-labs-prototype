What we covered and how to apply it this week.

What We Covered

Your personal brand exists whether you've defined it or not. In this session, you ran a three-step process to take control of it: customize a set of interview questions for your background, answer them honestly to build a real dataset, and run a brand exploration prompt to generate your brand document. The output - purpose, mission, values, tone, differentiators, and brand voice guidelines - is yours to use, store, and share.

---

Try This Week

Take your Brand Voice Guidelines section and paste it as a custom instruction in ChatGPT or Claude. Every response you get from that point forward will start sounding more like you. It takes 30 seconds to set up and it changes how every future output reads.
